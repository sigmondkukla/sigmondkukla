Brick Attack

Brick Attack is my VR rendition of the classic game Atari Breakout. Created after Crop Craze, it is much simpler in scope and concept. When creating Brick Attack, I focused on learning a few new things. First, Brick Attack is built using VRIF, a framework for quickly creating complex interactions. This allowed me to get off the ground fast and incorporate more advanced interactions, such as physics-based buttons and a grabbable slider to control the paddle. I also learned how to connect to the Oculus Platform for leaderboards and achievements, giving Brick Attack some addtional depth and allowing players to compete with their friends.

Brick Attack is available on the Oculus Store for Meta Quest.
